Classic Cheese Tart Recipe!

Whether you are hosting a party or just craving a sweet treat for yourself, this recipe is perfect to satisfy your needs. Follow along to find out exactly how this family recipe is made.

Ingredients List:

My To Do List

Add
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 12 vanilla wafers
  • 1 can cherry pie filling

Steps:

My To Do List

Add
  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit
  • Place cupcake liner in each cup
  • Beat cream cheese, sugar, vanilla, & eggs together
  • Place a vanilla wafer in each cup
  • Pour 1 spoon of mixture over each wafer
  • Bake for 20 minutes
  • Chill thoroughly

Serve and Enjoy!
